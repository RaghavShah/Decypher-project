Learn how to make maggi


Material requied line

  • one bowl
  • 1 packet of maggi
  • 2 cup water
  • some veggies
  • butter
  • chees

Steps to prepare maggi

  1. Take a bowl and boil 2 cup of waters
  2. Add maggi in boiling water
  3. Add maggi masala
  4. Add some butter
  5. Add veggies like :
    • capcicum
    • green chillies
    • onion
  6. wait for 5 min
  7. Serve in a bowl and add chees on it
  8. YOUR MAGGI IS READY
